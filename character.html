Backstory

Dylan grew up in a crumbling city where sirens was the lullaby of the city and trust was a rare token.

From a young age, dylan saw the system fail on those around him and friends gone missing from a systeem that was corruption and kept left crimes unsolved. He didn’t grow up dreaming of becoming a detective. He became detective to solved because no one else would would solve. with an eye for every detail the uncanny ability to read people, he quickly rose through the ranks of a broken system, he would never played by the book. Preferred to do alley whispers, and falsify any official reports believed that truth often hid where the law refused to look.

Dylan is well known for his quietly intense with the layered presence, Dylan walks the moral grey line. He secretly works on cases that others won't touch gang setups, political silences, and he does it with a calm precision that unnerves both allies and enemies.

Haunted by one unsolved case from his early years, a close friend of his who vanished one day without a trace. dylan would never stops chasing answers, even when it costs him sleep and his sanity, will keep looking until he is done.

Despite the hardened exterior, Dylan carries empathy like a second weapon used carefully but always ready. He trusts few, loves quietly, and rarely lets down his guard, but those who earn his loyalty find in him a fierce protector who'd burn the world to keep them safe.

Dylan doesn’t just solve mysteries. He becomes them.
